### Week 8: Casting and Molding This week's assignment was to make and mold some things. For the design part, I decided to go with shopbot instead of the 3d bot because this seemed more understandable to me. I designed a person thing in fusion and cut him out (outer layer was full cuts, eyes and mouth were pocket cuts): ![video of embroidery](shopslice.MOV) ![image of embroidery](shopbotman.jpg) Next was the mold/cast part. I decided to 3d print this uno card from thingiverse (credit to naheeln17 at https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:5412776/files): ![video of embroidery](uno3dprint.MOV) ![image of embroidery](unocard.jpg) Then, with some pieces to hold it in place, I molded it around a silicon mixture for over a day: ![image of embroidery](originalmold.jpg) After a day, it had solidified and I had a place to cast some wax mixture of Part A and Part B: ![image of embroidery](finalmold.jpg) ![image of embroidery](waxcast.jpg)
